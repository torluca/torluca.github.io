About Me

I am a post-doctoral researcher at the University Observatory (USM) of the Ludwig Maximilians University (LMU) of Munich (Germany). My research interests lie at the interface between galaxy evolution and cosmology. I am involved in modelling the galaxy population in the Universe and how image and spectra simulators map the intrinsic properties of galaxies into those that we see through the telescopes. This will help in providing the most stringent constraints to date on the model that describes our Universe and shed light on the nature of its dark component and the galaxies that live in it.

I am also heavily involved in science communication. I am a freelance collaborator for the Italian outreach platform "Geopop" with which I have been collaborating since March 2023, authoring more than 170 articles on Astronomy and Space exploration, totalling more than 3 million reads.
